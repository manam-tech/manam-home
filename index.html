Welcome to Manam

A place where compassion meets professional care.

At Manam Elders Home, we understand that growing older comes with its own set of challenges. Our mission is to provide a home-like environment where seniors receive the respect, care, and companionship they deserve.

We are dedicated to enhancing the quality of life for our residents through personalized care plans, engaging activities, and nutritional support, all within a safe and secure facility.

  • 24/7 Professional Nursing Care
  • Home-Cooked Nutritious Meals
  • Social & Recreational Activities
